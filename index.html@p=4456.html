Rubber Flooring
Performance

– Premium Wear Resistance: High abrasion resistance and exceptional service life — ideal for high-traffic areas.
– Outstanding Eco-Performance: Made from natural rubber — PVC-free, phthalate-free, halogen-free, with low TVOC emissions for healthier indoor air.
– Heavy-Duty Impact & Pressure Resistance: Withstands heavy impact without surface dents, absorbs shock from sustained loads, supports forklift traffic, and resists repeated chair-wheel rolling.
– High Resilience: Outstanding elasticity provides comfortable underfoot feel, reducing fatigue from prolonged walking and standing.
– Exceptional Slip Resistance: R10 slip-resistance rating delivers reliable grip even on wet surfaces.
– Superior Fire & Cigarette-Burn Resistance: Compliant with ASTM and EU EN standards. Low smoke density, with cigarette-burn resistance of Class 3 or higher.
