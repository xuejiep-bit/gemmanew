GEMMANEW About GEMMANEW

### GEMMANEW

GEMMANEW is a Poland-imported brand and a leading integrated supplier of building decoration materials. Drawing on more than 80 years of craftsmanship and expertise from Poland's GEMMA Group, GEMMANEW officially entered the Chinese market in 2018 and is headquartered in Shenzhen. Our core business covers three product systems — flooring, wall panels, and doors — providing the global market with premium spatial materials and integrated space solutions. Backed by deep technical expertise and rigorous quality control, GEMMANEW holds ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 international certifications, along with Building Decoration & Renovation Professional Contractor qualifications and a Safety Production Licence — providing dual assurance for both product quality and project delivery. The company has been recognised as a High-Tech Enterprise, Specialised & Innovative SME, Science & Technology SME, and Innovative SME. With 6 invention patents and 18 utility-model patents, GEMMANEW ranks among the top 10 international brands in the resilient-flooring industry and is recognised by leading industry authorities for its overall strength. Backed by extensive project experience, GEMMANEW has built a portfolio of landmark cases across Healthcare, Education & Sports, Hospitality & Office, Residential & Commercial, Transportation, and Industrial sectors, with projects worldwide. Guided by our mission of "Contributing to a Greener, Lower-Carbon Earth," we combine European-imported quality with end-to-end service to deliver exceptional spatial value to our clients.

### GEMMANEW Core Technology Advantages

- 2009 — GEMMANEW became one of the first three manufacturers worldwide to master T-Class wear technology for homogeneous PVC flooring, establishing its industry leadership.

- 2012 — GEMMANEW pioneered the 1.2 mm ultra-wear-layer technology for multi-layer composite PVC flooring, once again ranking among the world's top three manufacturers and redefining industry standards for wear layers.

- 2020 — GEMMANEW became one of the first three manufacturers worldwide to custom-produce 3.0 mm homogeneous PVC flooring, delivering bespoke solutions for healthcare and other demanding professional sectors.

- 2021 — GEMMANEW launched its Carbon-Crystal Click Flooring, featuring a specialised densification process that resists cigarette burns without leaving marks — setting the industry benchmark for floor protection in high-traffic commercial spaces.

- 2022 — GEMMANEW joined the top three quartz-flooring companies, leading industry innovation through advanced material technology and continuously expanding new flooring applications.

- 2024 — GEMMANEW's second-generation rubber flooring delivered triple resistance to iodine, strong corrosion, and oil stains — performance that rivals top international brands such as Nora.
